FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Is my capital locked?

No. Equiflow is a non-custodial protocol. You maintain ownership and can withdraw at any time based on tier liquidity limits.

How do I withdraw my profits?

Withdraw directly to your connected Web3 wallet anytime. Processing takes under 5 minutes.

What are the platform fees?

Flat 1% annual management fee + 5% performance fee on profits only. If you don't earn, we don't earn.

Is my personal data safe?

Bank-grade encryption and decentralized architecture. We never access your private keys.

Are returns guaranteed?

No. All investing involves risk and past performance does not guarantee future results. Equiflow's AI is designed to optimise for growth while managing risk — but markets are unpredictable. Never invest more than you can afford to lose.

Every active investor with a confirmed deposit is automatically entered each month. No extra steps needed. Winner is selected randomly and announced live on our Telegram channel.
